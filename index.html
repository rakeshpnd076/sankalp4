  About Sankalp Cancer Care Foundation  

Dr. B. K. Singh
Hon. Executive Director

Sankalp Cancer Care Foundation (SCCF) was setup in 2018 by Mrs. Jayanti Singh after her close family member and a couple of friends suffered from this dreaded cancer disease and finally expired. Sankalp is a “Not for Profit Charitable Organization” and it’s Mission is to educate the masses about the scourge of various types of Cancers, their Prevention and undertake timely screening for various types of Cancer at the door steps of the underprivileged masses free of cost. If an early detection is made, Sankalp would be able to help such patients to seek timely treatment and intervention. The process of timely Screening helps find Cancers at an early and treatable stage and thus improves the victim's survival rate immensely.
